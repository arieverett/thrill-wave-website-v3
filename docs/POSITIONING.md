# Thrill Wave Positioning

## Identity

Strategic depth of a consultancy. Analytical power of an intelligence platform. Cinematic craft of a documentary house. One company.

Three pillars: **Research. Intelligence. Film.**

## What We Are

A strategic consulting and documentary production company. We find the story before we film it. We run open source intelligence on an organization's brand — audience sentiment, search behavior, competitive positioning, social commentary — and the gap between how the world sees you and how you see yourself becomes the brief. Then we produce the media.

## What We Are NOT

- Not a "video production company" — never use this phrase
- Not a tech company that happens to make video
- Not a content mill, social media agency, or event videography outfit
- Not the safe choice — the right choice

## How We Talk About Ourselves

### Lead with the insight, not the AI
The intelligence layer is a capability, not the headline. Nobody cares about the tool — they care about the edge it gives them. Say "we found something your competitors missed" not "we used AI to analyze your brand."

### SITREP
Our intelligence product. Open source intelligence (OSINT) applied to brand perception.

Two layers:
- **External**: Public web scan — social sentiment, search behavior, news coverage, competitor positioning, Reddit/YouTube/forum commentary
- **Internal**: Proprietary org knowledge under NDA — internal comms, stakeholder interviews, strategic docs

The gap between external perception and internal reality = the brief.

SITREP can be sold standalone ($5K-$10K) or bundled as the discovery phase of a production contract.

### Copy Rules
- No em dashes in headlines
- Never use "content" — use media, film, video, or story
- Never say "failing" when describing a prospect's current state
- Never promise ROI
- Don't say "like consultants" — we ARE consultants
- Don't lead with AI or technology
- Brand aesthetic: retro sci-fi, analog warmth, CRT-in-a-dark-room feeling

## Competitive Edge

1. **Intelligence-first process** — we research before we write. Every other production company asks "what do you want to make?" Nobody asks "what should you make?" That's the gap where budget gets wasted.
2. **Willingness to go where others don't** — controversial subjects, politically sensitive work, stories most crews won't touch. We don't flinch.
3. **AI-augmented speed** — lean 3-person team that operates at the speed of a much larger shop because we've automated the intelligence and research layer.
4. **Genuine craft obsession** — drone, cable cam, high-speed, documentary-grade production. Not just a camera on a tripod.
5. **Lean overhead** — no bloated agency structure. The people you talk to are the people who make the work.

## Positioning by Vertical

### Government / Public Affairs
"You're spending public money on communications that aren't reaching the public. We can show you exactly where the gap is before you spend another dollar on media that doesn't move anyone."

### Healthcare
"Your patient stories are powerful, but they're not reaching the people who need to hear them. We find the gap between how your community sees you and the story you're actually trying to tell."

### Defense / Aerospace
"Recruitment content that looks like every other defense contractor won't solve your talent pipeline problem. We figure out what the workforce is actually saying about you online — then we build media that speaks to reality, not the brochure."

### Tribal Nations
"Your stories have been told by outsiders for too long. We build the intelligence layer first — what's being said, where it's being said, who's listening — so the media we create actually reaches the people who need to hear it."

### NGOs
"You're doing work that matters in places most people can't find on a map. Documentary-grade storytelling that cuts through the noise, grounded in intelligence about what your audience actually cares about."
