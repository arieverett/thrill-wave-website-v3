## Flagship

**ITCA (Inter Tribal Council of Arizona)** — Three engagements, three subjects. Each one required pre-production intelligence that changed the creative direction.
- WIC Nutrition Services: Needed to increase enrollment. SITREP revealed tribal members associated WIC with government surveillance. Barrier was trust, not awareness. Produced: tribal mothers speaking direct to camera, trust-first framing.
- Tribal Education Center (TEC): New facility needed positioning. SITREP revealed what mattered was sovereignty over education, not the building. Reframed from facility tour to sovereignty story.
- Voter Engagement ("Your Voice, Your Power"): Low tribal voter turnout. SITREP surfaced suppression history, redistricting, poll access barriers. Ten episodes. In production.
- Current contract: $84,150 docu-series through July 2026.

**Relentless Beats** — Visual media strategy and live event coverage for almost a decade. Festival/concert promoter (Goldrush, Decadence AZ). Long-term relationship, not a one-off.

## What the Website Needs Per Featured Client

For the 3-4 pieces featured on the homepage:
- Client name (or "Government agency in Arizona" if unnamed)
- One sentence: what the SITREP / research revealed
- One sentence: what was produced as a result
- One strong frame or still from the work

## What We DON'T Feature

- Work that was purely execution (client told us what to make, we made it)
- Work where the insight/research angle isn't clear
- Anything that looks like event videography, social clips, or highlight reels — that's not the positioning
