The Problem — 01

Most video production starts with the wrong question.

"What should we make?" is asked after the budget is approved, not before. The result is media built on assumptions instead of intelligence.

Organizations spend $50K–$500K on video annually and most of it underperforms. Not because the production quality is bad, but because the strategic foundation is missing. The wrong story, told to the wrong audience, through the wrong channel. We built the SITREP to fix that.
